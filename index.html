Meet Luna the Otterly Adorable Sea Pup
Luna the otter pup, formerly know as Pup 681, was abandoned by her mother and found all alone at a California beach in early November 2014. She is now exploring the habitat in her new home, the Shedd Aquarium.
Luna has already mastered swimming on her tummy and back, according to the aquarium, and can dive. She can now use her flipper to play and propel her around the special 1,249-gallon private nursery pool she calls home. She's so adorable!!
